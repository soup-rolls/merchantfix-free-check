Sample findings

Public-page evidence gaps.

Priority 1

Shipping policy link is not visible from the product page.

Observed evidence: The product page footer and product-detail area do not expose a reachable shipping or delivery policy link in the sampled view.

Suggested edit: Add a clear shipping policy link near the footer and product information area. The page should describe delivery regions, timing, cost logic, and exceptions in visible customer-facing text.

Priority 2

Return/refund wording is present but hard to locate.

Observed evidence: A return page exists, but it is not linked from the inspected product page or customer support area.

Suggested edit: Link the return/refund policy from product, footer, and support paths. Keep the wording consistent with customer-facing checkout expectations.

Priority 3

Business contact evidence is thin.

Observed evidence: The page shows a generic contact form but no visible support email, business identity, or response expectation in the sampled public view.

Suggested edit: Add a reachable support email or contact method, expected response time, and business identity details where appropriate.
